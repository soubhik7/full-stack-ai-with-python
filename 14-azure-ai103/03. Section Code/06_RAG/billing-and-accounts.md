# CloudXeus Technology Services — Billing and Accounts FAQ

## How do I update my payment method?
Sign in to the CloudXeus Customer Portal, go to Account > Billing >
Payment Methods, and select "Add Payment Method." New payment methods
are verified within 15 minutes. Your next invoice will be charged to
the default payment method on file.

## When am I billed for my services?
CloudXeus bills monthly in arrears. Invoices are generated on the 1st
of each month for the previous month's usage and are due within 30
days. Enterprise customers on annual contracts are billed annually
in advance.

## How do I get a copy of an invoice?
All invoices are available in the Customer Portal under Account >
Billing > Invoice History. Invoices can be downloaded as PDF and
are retained for seven years.

## What happens if my payment fails?
If a payment fails, CloudXeus retries the charge after 3 days and
again after 7 days. You will receive an email notification after
each failed attempt. Services are suspended if payment is not
received within 21 days of the invoice due date.

## How do I cancel a service?
Submit a cancellation request in the Customer Portal under Account >
Services > Manage. Cancellations take effect at the end of the
current billing cycle. Monthly plans have no cancellation fee.
Annual contracts cancelled early are subject to an early termination
fee of 25% of the remaining contract value.

## Can I get a refund?
Refunds are available within 30 days of initial signup for new
customers under the CloudXeus Satisfaction Guarantee. After 30 days,
refunds are issued only for verified billing errors or SLA credits.
