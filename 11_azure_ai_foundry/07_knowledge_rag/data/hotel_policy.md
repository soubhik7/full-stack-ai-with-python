# Crystal Hotels — Guest Policy Reference

## Cancellation Policy
Reservations may be cancelled free of charge up to 72 hours before check-in.
Cancellations within 72 hours of check-in are charged one night's rate plus tax.
No-shows are charged the full reservation amount.

## Pet Policy
Crystal Hotels welcomes up to 2 pets per room, maximum 50 lbs combined weight.
A non-refundable pet fee of $75 per stay applies, regardless of stay length.
Service animals are exempt from the pet fee.

## Early/Late Checkout
Standard checkout is 12:00 PM. Late checkout until 2:00 PM is available on request
at no charge, subject to availability. Checkout after 2:00 PM is billed at 50% of
one night's rate.

## Loyalty Program
Crystal Circle members earn 12 points per dollar spent on room rates.
Redeeming 25,000 points grants one free standard-room night, subject to availability.
